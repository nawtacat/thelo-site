What is
thelo?

thelo is a handwriting-first learning platform for math.

Students solve problems using a pen and tablet, not a keyboard.

Lessons are built from interactive stages with audio, animated handwriting, and instant feedback.

Why another platform?

Real Handwriting

Students draw equations the way they do on paper — muscle-memory beats multiple-choice.

Instant Feedback

Google Vision grades answers and nudges the learner forward.

Integrated Notes

Take notes directly on the platform with your pen — quick, easy, and always accessible during the lesson.

Live Demo

Play with a SAT-style algebra lesson. Press A to switch ink, write your answer in blue, hit Check.

Future Vision

thelopad

Thelopad is a low-cost drawing tablet designed specifically for education. Unlike generic graphics tablets, it’s tailored for platforms like thelo that rely on handwriting input for math and STEM learning. Compact, affordable, and plug-and-play, thelopad aims to make active digital learning accessible in low-resource environments—starting in Armenia and expanding globally through institutional partnerships.
