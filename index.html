Why another learning tool?

Most e-learning platforms are passive: watch a video, click “next”. Students stay silent, teachers can’t see the thought-process, and attention slips away.

✏️ Real handwriting

Students draw equations the way they do on paper — muscle-memory beats multiple-choice.

⚡ Instant AI feedback

Google Vision + custom logic grade answers in <1 s and nudge the learner forward.

🎮 Gamified journey

Points, sounds, chat reactions — tiny dopamine loops that keep motivation high.

🎙️ Teacher narration

Animated stroke-replays synced with voice make abstract ideas crystal-clear.

🌐 Armenian-first

Full UI + content localisation so rural schools can leapfrog without language barriers.

🛠️ Serverless & scalable

Firebase Auth, Functions, and Netlify hosting — nothing to install, nothing to patch.

🎯 Live Demo

Play with a SAT-style algebra lesson. Press A to switch ink, write your answer in blue and hit Check.

How it works

  1. 1

    Teacher records

    They draw on our canvas, speak, and export a lesson.json + .ogg.

  2. 2

    Student watches

    Animated strokes + narration re-play in their browser, bandwidth-friendly.

  3. 3

    Student writes

    They toggle blue ink, solve the problem, click Check.

  4. 4

    AI grades

    Canvas snapshot → Cloud Function → Google Vision → feedback in <700 ms.
